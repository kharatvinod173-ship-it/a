Hospital Revenue and Billing Analysis


Developed an interactive Power BI-style dashboard using SQL and data visualization techniques to analyze healthcare revenue, department-wise billing performance, payment status, monthly revenue trends, and patient billing breakdown.

Key Features:
• Revenue KPI tracking and performance monitoring
• Department-wise billing analysis
• Payment status tracking (Paid, Pending, Overdue)
• Monthly revenue trend analysis
• Patient billing distribution and insights
• Interactive filters and drill-down reporting

Tools Used:
SQL, Power BI, Excel, DAX, Data Visualization

Key Business Insight
Cardiology contributes the highest share of revenue, generating 34% of total revenue (₹4.2 Cr), but also records the highest outstanding dues of ₹48 Lakhs, primarily due to delays in insurance claim processing.
Oncology demonstrates the strongest financial performance with a 91.2% collection rate, indicating efficient billing and payment recovery processes.
Recommendation: Implement an automated insurance reconciliation and claim-tracking system within the Cardiology department to accelerate claim settlements and potentially recover ₹25–30 Lakhs in the upcoming quarter (Q1).

Cardiology is the largest revenue-generating department but faces significant collection challenges due to insurance claim delays. By adopting automated insurance reconciliation, the hospital can improve cash flow and recover an estimated ₹25–30 Lakhs in Q1. Meanwhile, Oncology serves as a benchmark with its industry-leading 91.2% collection rate.
